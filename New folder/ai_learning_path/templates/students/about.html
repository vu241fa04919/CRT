About the Platform

Personalized education powered by intelligent recommendations

Our Mission

In today's digital age, the amount of technical study content is overwhelming. Students often find themselves trapped in "tutorial hell," shifting between books and video courses without clear directions or practical build exercises.

The **AI Learning Path Generator** was designed to solve this by providing custom-tailored curriculum sequences. Based on your current education, skills you already know, target career, and daily time constraints, we build a weekly program matching your learning style.

Adaptive Duration

Our engine automatically compresses the path into 4 weeks for students with high availability, or expands it to 8 weeks for students balancing multiple studies, adjusting workloads accordingly.

Gamification Rules

Earn 50 Experience Points (XP) for every weekly module checked off. Unlock progression badges like "Consistency Champ" and rank on the student leaderboard.

System Details

  • Designed using the Python Django web framework and SQLite.
  • Responsive frontend featuring glassmorphism styles and interactive animations.
  • Rule-based NLP counselor chatbot assistant for general student guidance.
